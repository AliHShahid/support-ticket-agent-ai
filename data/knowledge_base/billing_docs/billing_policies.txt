BILLING POLICIES AND PROCEDURES

1. SUBSCRIPTION PLANS
- Basic Plan: $29.99/month - Up to 1,000 API calls
- Professional Plan: $99.99/month - Up to 10,000 API calls  
- Enterprise Plan: $299.99/month - Unlimited API calls

2. BILLING CYCLES
- All subscriptions are billed monthly on the anniversary date of signup
- Charges appear 1-2 business days before the billing date
- Failed payments result in service suspension after 3 days

3. REFUND POLICY
- Full refunds available within 30 days of initial purchase
- Partial refunds may be considered for service outages exceeding 24 hours
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

4. PAYMENT METHODS
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- PayPal
- Bank transfers for Enterprise customers

5. BILLING DISPUTES
- Contact billing support within 60 days of charge
- Provide transaction ID and detailed explanation
- Disputes are resolved within 10 business days

6. ACCOUNT SUSPENSION
- Accounts are suspended for non-payment after 7 days
- Data is retained for 30 days after suspension
- Reactivation requires payment of outstanding balance plus $25 reactivation fee
